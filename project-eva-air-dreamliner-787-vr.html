EVA AIR DREAMLINER 787 VR (2018)

- immersive premium flight showcase promoting the new Boeing 787-

Overview

The EVA Air Dreamliner 787 VR experience was created to promote EVA Air's new Boeing 787 fleet. The project provides an immersive walkthrough of the aircraft's premium cabins, highlighting the luxury, comfort, and advanced features of the Dreamliner through high-fidelity VR.

My Role

I was responsible for the interactive experience design and production. My focus was on recreating the interior of the 787 with photographic accuracy, designing the interactive highlights of the seat features, and ensuring a smooth, high-end presentation for the brand's marketing events.

Highlights

  • Achieved high-fidelity architectural visualization of aircraft interiors for VR.
  • Designed interactive touchpoints for premium seat features and cabin lighting controls.
  • Optimized assets for mobile VR platforms to ensure portability for trade shows.
  • Established a production pipeline for photorealistic rendering in a real-time environment.

Art & Process

The core objective was "digital luxury." We focused on material accuracy—leather, wood, and metallic accents—using advanced texture mapping and baked lighting to create a space that felt truly premium and inviting within the VR headset.
