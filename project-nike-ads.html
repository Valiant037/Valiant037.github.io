Class Magazine: Nike Running Shoes Ads (2012)

- creative illustration and layout for specialized print media -

Overview

The Class Magazine: Nike Running Shoes Ads project involved creating a series of high-impact promotional illustrations for Class Magazine Issue 7. The objective was to present Nike's latest running footwear in a way that resonated with the magazine's urban, design-conscious audience, blending photographic elements with digital artwork.

My Role

Working as a digital artist and illustrator, I was responsible for the conceptualization and execution of the ad layouts. My focus was on creating dynamic compositions that emphasized the energy and technical features of the shoes, using a mix of digital painting, photo manipulation, and graphic design to achieve a premium "high-fashion" commercial look.

Highlights

  • Produced cover-grade illustrations for a specialized design magazine.
  • Developed high-impact visual compositions for a global brand (Nike).
  • Executed complex digital painting and photo-retouching workflows.
  • Established a cohesive visual style across multiple ad variations.

Art & Process

The visual strategy was "movement and energy." We utilized sharp, flowing lines and a high-contrast palette to simulate the sense of motion. Every highlight on the product was carefully retouched to emphasize the materials and textures, ensuring the shoe remained the hero of the composition.
