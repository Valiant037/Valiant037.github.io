Toyota Official Website Rendering (2019)

- high-fidelity 360 imagery pipeline for official marketing assets -

Overview

The Toyota Official Website Rendering project was a major undertaking to create high-fidelity, photorealistic 360-degree car renders for the official Taiwan Toyota website. The goal was to provide customers with an interactive, high-detail view of the car models from any angle, directly in their browser.

My Role

As Art Director, I established the production pipeline and rendering workflow to ensure consistency across the entire fleet of vehicles. I supervised the 3D modeling, lighting, and material setup to match Toyota's strict branding and photographic standards, while optimizing the assets for high-performance web display.

Highlights

  • Developed a high-efficiency 3D rendering pipeline for mass production of marketing assets.
  • Achieved photorealistic quality that matches physical advertising photography.
  • Coordinated between the art team and web developers to integrate 360 views seamlessly.

Art & Process

The challenge was balancing the large volume of renders with the need for extreme detail. We used advanced PBR (Physically Based Rendering) materials and custom lighting rigs for each model to ensure the paint, glass, and metal reflections felt realistic and dynamic.
