ROG CITADEL XV: SCAR RUNNER (2022)

- high-speed DLC with puzzle-driven routes -

Overview

ROG CITADEL XV: SCAR RUNNER is a high-speed expansion for the ROG Citadel XV experience. Unlike the main showroom, SCAR RUNNER focuses on rapid traversal, parkour-inspired movement, and puzzle-driven route optimization, challenging players to master the environment.

My Role

Returning as Game Director and Art Director, I focused on creating a distinct "speedrunning" feel. I designed the environmental cues and lighting that guide players through high-speed routes without losing visual clarity, while introducing new mechanics that expanded the base game's reach.

Highlights

  • Designed an intricate level layout optimized for high-speed parkour and verticality.
  • Developed environmental "signposting" techniques using lighting and motion.
  • Unified the DLC's hyper-modern sci-fi aesthetic with the established ROG brand identity.
  • Oversaw the balance between tight platforming and visual spectacle.

Art & Process

The visual strategy for SCAR RUNNER revolved around bold, high-contrast silhouettes. We wanted the player to always feel the tension of speed, using blurred light trails and dynamic weather effects to heighten the sense of motion.
