ROG CITADEL XV (2020)

- the foundational hybrid showroom and FPS experience for ASUS ROG -

Overview

The original ROG Citadel XV (2020) was a groundbreaking project for ASUS ROG, designed to elevate the traditional "virtual showroom" into a gamified narrative experience. It serves as a central hub where users can explore high-end hardware in a thematic sci-fi environment before transitioning into a fast-paced shooting gallery.

My Role

As Game Director and Art Director, I was responsible for the project's entire creative vision. I defined the futuristic art style, directed the level design to facilitate both product inspection and combat, and oversaw the integration of complex visual effects and lighting that won the project a Red Dot Award in 2021.

Highlights

  • Pioneered the hybrid "Showroom + Game" format for major tech hardware launches.
  • Winner of the 2021 Red Dot Award for high-quality communication design.
  • Designed an expansive, high-fidelity sci-fi world optimized for diverse PC hardware.
  • Managed a multi-disciplinary team to deliver a cohesive and polished launch title.

Art & Process

The visual goal was "Hard-Surface Elegance." We combined brutalist architecture with neon-lit high-tech accents to create a space that felt both intimidatingly powerful and invitingly modern—perfectly mirroring the ROG brand identity. Every product encounter was treated as a cinematic event, with custom lighting and sound design.
