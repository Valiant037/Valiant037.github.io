ROG CITADEL XV: OMNI (2022)

- top-down shooting arcade mini-game in the virtual showroom -

Overview

ROG CITADEL XV: OMNI is a mini arcade game found within the ROG virtual showroom. It allows players to roleplay as the ROG mascot robot OMNI in a fast-paced, top-down shooting gameplay experience. The game serves as a fun "meta" interaction that rewards visitors for exploring the main showroom's hidden corners.

My Role

I was the Game Designer and Artist for this project. I designed and created the stage layout, 3D models, lighting setups, visual effects, and customized UI. Additionally, I animated the short opening sequence that sets the tone for the retro-inspired arcade action.

Highlights

  • Designed an engaging top-down shooting mechanic within the Unity Engine.
  • Created stylized, high-performance 3D models and VFX for a distinct arcade look.
  • Integrated the mini-game seamlessly as an "in-universe" interactive terminal.
  • Handled everything from concept art and modeling to short-form animation.

Art & Process

The design of OMNI focused on "chunky," readable shapes and high-energy color palettes. Since the camera is positioned at a top-down angle, we emphasized strong silhouettes and emissive textures to ensure the action remained clear and satisfying.
