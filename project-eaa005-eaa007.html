Egg Attack: Star Wars Series Promotional Animation (2014)

- photorealistic 3D animation for high-end collectible figures -

Overview

The Egg Attack Star Wars Series promotional animation was created to showcase a line of high-end, stylized collectible figures. The project required a blend of the iconic Star Wars cinematic feel with the unique, high-detail aesthetic of the Egg Attack product line, resulting in a photorealistic presentation that highlights the craftsmanship of the physical products.

My Role

Working at Artlord Studio, my responsibilities spanned the entire 3D production pipeline. I handled the layout, camera/character animation, modeling, and texturing. I also focused on the lighting and simulation, using the Arnold Renderer to achieve photorealistic materials and cinematic atmospheric effects.

Highlights

  • Developed cinematic 3D animation for globally recognized IP (Star Wars).
  • Achieved high-end photorealistic rendering using Arnold Renderer.
  • Choreographed dynamic character animations that play into the stylized nature of the figures.
  • Managed the full technical pipeline from initial modeling to final composite.

Art & Process

The visual challenge was to make "toy" scale figures feel epic. We used narrow depth-of-field and rich, dramatic lighting to create a sense of weight and cinematic scale. Every scratch and metal texture was carefully authored to reflect the premium build quality of the physical collectibles.
