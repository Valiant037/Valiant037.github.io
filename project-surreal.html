Surreal Education (2016 - 2019)

- VR education platform that gamify educational materials into interactive experience -


Overview

Surreal Education is an educational VR platform designed to teach students knowledge through an immersive, gamified experience in VR. The hardware ranged from PCVR, standalone, to mobile devices. This continues project evolved throughout my tenue as an art director. The platform aims offers multiple VR experiences and learning materials that offers solution to schools or teachers to enhance students education experiences.

My Role

As Art Director for the Surreal Education platform, I am responsible supervised the art team and alongside to create a visually engaging yet educational VR contents for our audiences. I defined the stylistic direction with knowledge accurate information so that each content is filled with a vibrant, game-like aesthetic and oversaw the design of interactive assets and environmental lighting. I also was responsible for most of the promotional printed materials that is required during our exhibitions or campaign.

Highlights

  • Developed immersive educational environment focused on spatial learning.
  • Led the team to concept, design and created art assets such as models, textures, animations, arts, effects, and sounds required for all experiences.
  • Implemented interactivities with programmers for optimal VR engagement and feedbacks.
  • Supervise all art assets pipelines during production with in-house artists and outsource parties.
  • Proposal to potential clients for new contents and experiences.

Art & Process

Here is a brief overall of all the contents I supervised and created for the Surreal Education Platform



Project: Cell Factory | PCVR Cell Factory was designed to help players learn about the organic structure of a cell by assembling its components as if they were parts of a factory. The visual strategy focused on making the microscopic world feel both alive and approachable. To achieve this, we employed vibrant, fluorescent color palettes and fluid shaders to simulate the cytoplasmic environment, ensuring that every interaction felt dynamic, intuitive, and educationally meaningful.









Project: Atomic Trial | PCVR Atomic Trial was designed to help players learn about the composition of each elements from the periodic table. The goal is to focus on making the visual to support the engaging gameplay of catching 'photon', 'neutron' and 'electron'. The player will learn each of the element's composition in the periodic table that matches each of the flying orbs catched.

I created all the art assets in this experience and animated the opening sequence and elevator sequence in Maya and Unity. I also designed the key art and poster for the project.









Project: Vector Space | PCVR Vector Space is an experience suited for high school students that solves the problem of explaining abstract vector space problems in a virtual space. It aims to allow students to visualize 'vector space' much better in VR that allows them to memorize and understand the concept much faster and more efficient. I created all the arts and animation in this content.








Project: Jishan Islet | PCVR Jishan Islet is an experience school students to visit Jishan Islet for its unique geography structure for learning. The experience was created with the actual Jishan Islet photogrammetry scan that we aims to gives the best realistic immersive effect for our students.









Project: Teaching Cool | Vive Focus Teaching Cool is a platform that stores a library of 3D assets that is useful for students to visualize them in a classroom each with a Vive Focus headset. I supervised the creation of all the assets and UI design.





Project: CO2 | PCVR CO2 is the very first project I worked on in Shadoworks Studio. I was responsible for all the art assets, animations, sounds, and poster. It was one of the very first content in Surreal Platform as well. The style I chose was more towards cartoonish style that targeted younger audience.
