Echoes of Formosa (2024)

- historical action adventure set in 1640s Taiwan -

Overview

Echoes of Formosa is a story-driven action game that brings players back to 17th-century Taiwan, exploring the perilous land of Zeelandia through a blend of historical context, character-driven narrative, and modern game design.

My Role

As Game Director, I defined the core mechanics, story integration, scope, and production milestones, guiding the team from concept to final delivery. As Art Director, I set the visual language of the game—from key art and logo to UI, characters, and cinematics—and supervised the art team throughout production.

Highlights

  • Blends historical Zeelandia with a contemporary action game experience.
  • Unified art direction across environments, UI, characters, and cinematics.
  • Designed key visual, logo, and one of the intro cinematics to anchor the tone.
  • Built on Unity Engine with a production pipeline suitable for a Steam release.

Art & Process

This piece represents the visual foundation for Echoes of Formosa — from initial composition and lighting studies through to the final key art. In this section I showcase how the mood, color palette, and focal points evolved to balance historical storytelling with a modern action-game silhouette.
