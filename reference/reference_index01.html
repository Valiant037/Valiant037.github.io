About

Creative Visionary

Tien-Hung Chang (Dean) is an Art Director with over 15 years of experience in interactive media, game development, and immersive experiences.

Currently serving as Art Director at Moonshine Studio, Dean leads a team of interactive artists and has directed multiple award-winning projects including games, VR/MR experiences, and large-scale interactive exhibitions.
