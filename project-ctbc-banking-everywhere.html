CTBC: Banking Everywhere (2023)

- interactive virtual banking experience -

Overview

CTBC: Banking Everywhere is an interactive exhibition designed to showcase the future of digital banking. The project utilizes immersive technology to create a seamless, virtual banking environment where users can experience financial services in a futuristic setting.

My Role

Contributing as a lead artist and director, my focus was on the visual narrative and the design of the interactive touchpoints. I ensured the aesthetic aligned with CTBC's branding while pushing the boundaries of traditional banking interfaces into a more engaging, spatial experience.

Highlights

  • Developed an intuitive interactive system for virtual banking walkthroughs.
  • Created high-fidelity architectural visualizations for the virtual showroom.
  • Designed responsive UI elements that bridge the physical and digital space.
  • Optimized real-time rendering performance for large-scale displays.

Art & Process

The design language focused on transparency, flow, and modern aesthetics. By using clean lines and a professional blue color palette, we created a space that feels safe and trustworthy yet innovative and cutting-edge.
