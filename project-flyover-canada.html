FlyOver Canada: Soar with Santa (2014)

- special Christmas edition iRide previs and layout -

Overview

FlyOver Canada: Soar with Santa is a special Christmas-themed edition of the world-renowned FlyOver Canada flight simulation ride. The experience takes guests on a magical journey across the Canadian landscape to the North Pole, blending live-action aerial footage with whimsical digital characters and effects.

My Role

While working at Artlord Studio, I was responsible for the layout and previz of this special edition. My tasks included choreographing the camera movements to match the dynamic motion of the iRide theater, timing the digital elements with the physical flight path, and assisting with the general art direction to ensure a cohesive holiday atmosphere.

Highlights

  • Choreographed complex camera flight paths for a 4D theater experience.
  • Developed high-detail previs sequences that guided the final VFX production.
  • Integrated digital characters (Santa, elves) into live-action aerial plates.
  • Collaborated on the overall visual tone and pacing of the Christmas narrative.

Art & Process

The challenge was ensuring that the virtual flight felt natural and exciting when translated to the physical iRide motion base. We used high-fidelity previs to test G-forces and visual transitions, making sure every turn and dive felt immersive and magical for guests of all ages.
