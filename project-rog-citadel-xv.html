ROG Citadel XV (2020)

- virtual showroom that transforms into a fast-paced FPS experience -

Overview

ROG Citadel XV started as an interactive virtual showroom for ASUS ROG hardware and grew into a full FPS game experience with collectibles, DLC, and mini-games, designed to reward exploration while showcasing products in context.

My Role

As Game Director and Art Director, I shaped the overall concept, gameplay flow, and production roadmap, while defining the visual identity, level mood, and storytelling. I supervised level design, art assets, animation, UI, VFX, and QA to keep both gameplay and branding aligned.

Highlights

  • Hybrid experience that functions as both a showroom and a full FPS game.
  • Multiple chapters, DLC, and mini-games delivered within a unified art direction.
  • Strong integration of ROG product storytelling into environmental and gameplay beats.
  • Built in Unity Engine with optimization for wide PC hardware configurations.
